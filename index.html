Overview

Foundation models and Generative AI have in recent years shown great promise in unlocking agents that can exist in open worlds. Particularly, we have seen a rapid adoption of long-horizon planning in LLMs that take broad, often ambiguous objectives, and use context to reason, (partially) execute, and reconsider the next steps of the plan.

This competition, leveraging tools developed as part of DARPA TIAMUT progream, aims to provide a platform to researchers to evaluate behaviors of open-world, physically grounded AI agents, on tasks that require long-horizon planning and reasoning.
